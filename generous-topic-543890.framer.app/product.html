How It Works

We make data accessible and actionable for teams of all sizes.

Track

Before starting, the athlete opens the BarCoded app and enters their workout details. Once they hit 'Start,' the app does the rest, automatically capturing the motion data from every single rep. Whether they are in a facility or training on their own, their workout is being logged, providing a complete picture of their effort.

  • Simple for Athletes: An intuitive setup takes less than 10 seconds.

Holistic Data: Capture every training session, not just the ones you can personally supervise.





Analyze

Data is instantly synced to the cloud, where both the coach and athlete can view key performance metrics in our intuitive dashboard.

The moment a workout is complete, the data is processed and translated into objective, readable movement data. Coaches can view key metrics like barbell velocity, acceleration, and power output for their athletes and provide immediate feedback on their athletes’ performance, creating a powerful learning loop.

  • Instant Feedback: No more waiting to meet the coach again; feedback can be sent remotely.

  • Shared Visibility: Creates a powerful, transparent connection between coach and athlete.

Optimize

This is where the magic happens. Armed with objective data, such as velocity, acceleration and power, you can move beyond guesswork. Make informed decisions about when to increase the load, when to focus on recovery, and how to provide precise, personalized feedback that truly makes a difference.

  • Data-Driven Decisions: Replace subjective feelings with objective facts.

  • Peak Performance: Manage fatigue, make smarter workout decisions, and create the optimal path for every athlete to succeed.
